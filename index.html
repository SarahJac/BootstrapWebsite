Awards


American Music Awards

BTS bagged three awards at the AMAs 2021. The group won the award for the favourite pop group, for pop song and artist of the year.

Billboard Music Awards

BTS extended their reign over BBMAs 2021, as they scooped up four prizes, the most for the group at the event.

MNet Asian Music Awards

BTS swept all four Daesangs (grand prizes) including Song of the Year, Album of the Year, Worldwide Icon of the Year and Artist of the Year.

Melon Music Awards

BTS took home five awards at MMA 2021, including Song of the Year.
